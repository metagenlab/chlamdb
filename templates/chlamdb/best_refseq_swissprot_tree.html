Phylogeny including all orthologs and their closest hits in RefSeq. This phylogeny includes the 4 best Diamond RefSeq hit of each ortholog (excluding hits against sequences classified as part of the PVC). It was built with FastTree with default parameters. PVC sequences are colored in red while other sequences are colored according to the phylum. The tree was rooted by midpoint rooting.
