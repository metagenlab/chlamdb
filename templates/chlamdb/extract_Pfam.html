Info about the analysis

Get the list of Pfam domains shared between genomes (selected in panel A) and absent in others (selected in panel B).
It is possible to distinguish between plasmids and chromosomes by clicking on the dedicated checkbox.
Missing data: if more than one genome is selected in panel A, it is possible to tolerate missing data in 1 up to 10 genomes. In this way Pfam domains present in at least x out of y genomes selected are kept.
